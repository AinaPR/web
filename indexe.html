Music made easy

Musical Bio

She begins her violin studies at the age of 7 in the Victoria dels Àngels Music School (Sant Cugat del Vallès) with Sara Bolaños and Carles Miró. She continues her music training in Conservatori del Liceu (Barcelona) doing her performance degree in classical and contemporary music with Mauro Rossi, Daniel Cubero, Manel Porta and Gisella Curtolo. She also receives classes from Kai Gleusteen, Gerard Claret, Evelio Tieles and Aleksandar Krapovski as well as doing courses with Aram Asatryan (Madrid) and Chistian Ostertag (Horn, Austria).

Pedagogy

In the pedagogy area she took the course Teaching the Violin and Viola: Creating a healthy foundation of the Northwestern University (Illinois, USA) and did her Suzuki violin teacher training with Ana Podhajska (Sant Cugat).

In 2016 graduates in clinical Psychology in the Open University of Catalonia. She does her final project about violin teaching with kids with developmental Dyslexia.



Professional Activity

She has worked as a violin teacher in Espaiart and Artcàdia Music School (Castellar del Vallès) and Dinamic Crescendo (Rubí). Since 2018, she teaches at Can Fargues Music School (Barcelona) teaching both violin and music theory. In 2019 she began working in Fusió Dance and Music School (Sant Cugat) teaching Suzuki violin with kids from age 3.

Nowadays she combines her pedagogic activity with the performance with several orchestras such as Orquestra Terres de Marca (Igualada), Orquestra Batuelo (Badalona) and since 2016 is the concertino of the Youth Orchestra of Cerdanyola (JOC).
